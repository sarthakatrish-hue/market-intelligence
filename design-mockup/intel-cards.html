Where Calibre Wins
Photo logging vs barcode paywall
MFP locked its barcode scanner behind premium in Oct 2022. Calibre's photo-based logging requires no paywall and no manual scanning — the exact gap MFP users are complaining about.
→ Lead paid acquisition copy with "log meals by photo, no paywall" directly against MFP churners.
No pricing controversy baggage
MFP doubled premium to $79.99/year in Mar 2023, triggering sustained Reddit-visible churn. Calibre has no legacy pricing baggage to defend.
→ Position Calibre as the clean-slate alternative — no surprise price hikes, no feature lockouts.
Where They're Ahead
Database scale: 14M+ food items
MFP's food database is the largest in the category — 14M items, Vitess/PlanetScale infrastructure, and entries dating back 15+ years. This is a genuine moat.
→ Acknowledge the gap honestly. Compete on AI accuracy for common foods, not database breadth.
Brand recognition and installed base
MFP is the default answer when anyone searches "calorie tracking app." Calibre has no brand equity yet in this category.
→ Target acquisition at the moment of MFP frustration — intercept churn, don't fight awareness.
